Mock Interview — Practice Deck

1) 60‑second overview

Who you are → solution areas → proof metrics → vision. Use the opener below; keep to ≤60s.

2) Core solution areas & why

Security, Infra, App Innovation: tie to regional demand and customer pains; add one result metric per area.

3) Recent delivery win (use STAR)

Situation, Task, Action, Result with numbers. Prepare 3 variants (security, infra, app).

4) Security approach

Zero‑Trust: verify explicitly (MFA/CA), least privilege (PIM), assume breach (XDR+SIEM), secure dev (DevSecOps).

5) ICP & pipeline

Regions, buyer persona, typical land-and-expand motion, quarter pipeline signal.

6) Growth plan & differentiation

Designations, marketplace, co‑sell readiness; repeatable blueprints; time‑to‑value; training capability.

7) What do you want from Microsoft?

Co‑sell guidance, engineering hours for PoVs, marketplace amplification, field introductions.

Smart Questions to Ask Microsoft

Which regional plays are top‑priority this quarter? Fastest path to co‑sell readiness for us? Preferred marketplace + case‑study format?
